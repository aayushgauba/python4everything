About the Conference

Python 4 Almost Everyone is the first STL Python conference built for the whole local community.

From students and new developers to experienced professionals, everyone is welcome to attend, learn, and share.

We especially encourage attendees from every background to submit talks and help shape a practical, inclusive program for St. Louis.
